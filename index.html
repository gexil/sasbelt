SASbelt

SASbelt is a python program developed by G. Exil and K. Wimalan (Laboratoire Léon-Brillouin, Saclay, France)

from the MemProt program developed by J. Perez and A. Koutsioubas (Act Cryst D71:86, 2015).

This program models the low resolution shape of the amphiphilic belt of a membrane protein

using SAXS experimental data of the belt/protein complex and a PDB file of the membrane protein structure.
